My Experience with 4-H
Mission
The stated goal of 4-H is to develop citizenship, leadership, responsibility and life skills of youth through experiential learning programs in agriculture and a positive youth development approach.
Personal Connection
This is a picture of the farm I grew up on, where my family has raised cattle, pigs, sheep, horses, goats, crops, and one donkey, while my siblings and I participated in 4-H.
An Important Note
Some people may mistakenly view 4-H as an extension to the inhumane factory-farming systems, but the truth is the opposite: 4-H trains everyday kids to be knowledgeable about agriculture so that they can conduct local, small-scale, and humane farming practices as an alternative to corporate farms.
